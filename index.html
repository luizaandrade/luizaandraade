Luíza Andrade

I am a Data Coordinator in the Impact Evaluation department of the Development Research Group (DIME), where I help researchers ensure quality and reproducibility of their data work. My work focuses on disseminating best practices for data management and use of statistical software, particularly R and Stata. I also support projects with the adoption of new data sources and tools, and create public goods to make data work resources accessible to the development research community.
