Luíza Andrade

I am a data scientist specializing in social science research transparency and reproducibility. I create products that make high-quality data accessible to development researchers and policy makers. In a former life, I was trained as an environmental economist.

I work in the Development Impact Evaluation department (DIME) at the World Bank Group, in a team called DIME Analytics. My work focuses on promoting research transparency and reproducibility practices through trainings and code review. I also contribute to DIME's portfolio of impact evaluations by incorporating non-traditional data sources such as high-frequency crowdsourced and web-scraped data into research projects.
