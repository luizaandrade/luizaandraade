Development research in practice course

7-week online course teaching users of development data how to handle data effectively, efficiently, and ethically. The course covered the data workflow at each stage of an empirical research project, from design to analysis to publication. Held on summer 2021.

Course materials Course videos

Introduction to R

6-session training designed to familiarize participants with common research data tasks using R, including as data cleaning, visualization and analysis. Designed for an audience who is already familiar with another statistical programming language.

Course materials GitHub repository

Introduction to Stata

1-week training introducing the foundational functions in Stata for data analysis. Designed for participants with no previous coding experience. Last delivered in Croatia on October 2021.

Course materials GitHub repository

Introduction à la collecte de données de recherche

1-day training meant to expose participants to electronic data collection methods and to familiarize them with survey programming and data collection through ODK-based platforms. Delivered in Burkina Faso on March 2022.

Data management for public servants

Introductory sessions discussing how to approach and plan data work tasks. Delivered to civil servants in Ghana on February 2022 as part of the Bureacracy Lab's HR Analytics training.
